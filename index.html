About Us

Kicksmandu is an online sneaker store which first started in 2019. Our goal is to provide the most fresh and hype sneakers in the sneakerhead community in Nepal. WIth the growing sneaker community in Nepal it is very difficult for them to get sneakers as most retail stores in Nepal don't have new releases and there are no resellers. We are trying to change that and provide fresh kicks to all the sneakerheads in Nepal at good prices.
